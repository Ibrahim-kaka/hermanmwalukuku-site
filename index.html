Introduction to Training Firms.

Although Kenya is a strong leader in mobile technology, most young Kenyans still have a limited access to computers and the Internet. That is another thing I (Herman Mwalukuku) am attempting to change through participating in the introduction of Training Companies.

I have already established in Marungu Secondary School three Training Firms, Mshindi KCB Training Firm, which is in partnership with Kenya Commercial Bank, Green Bird Insurance Company which partners with Madison Insurance Company and Rainbow Printing Company which partners with Worl Life Works. The KCB, Madison and World Life Works have remained our mentors since 2011 and they give us necessary materialsl and training that we require.

I would like to give credit to Slovak teachers for their continued support as far as Training Firms are concerned
